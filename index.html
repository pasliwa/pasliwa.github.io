Multilayer Networks for Integration of Molecular Datasets



About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Oxford, working under the supervision of Prof. Julian Knight, Prof. Gesine Reinert and Prof. Heather Harrington. My research focuses on developing methods to find molecular subtypes of diseases, specifically I employ multilayer networks and community detection methods to integrate multimodal health datasets with examples in COVID-19 and sepsis. I am passionate about finding hidden structure in data and using it to better understand how we can help patients.

Research Interests

  • Networks Science
  • Machine Learning
  • Multi-omics
  • Molecular data integration
  • Acute immune response to infection
